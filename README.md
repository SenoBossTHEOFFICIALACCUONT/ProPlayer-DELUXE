# ProPlayer DELUXE

The media player you deserve. **ProPlayer DELUXE** is a high-fidelity, cross-platform media solution built with **ElectronJS**. Experience flawless, original-quality playback and stunning, customizable aesthetics inspired by **Apple Music** and **Spotify**. It's designed to give you ultimate control over your video and audio library.


## Features That Stand Out

**ProPlayer DELUXE** goes beyond basic playback, offering a suite of features focused on compatibility, customization, and high-end user experience.


### ðŸŽ¨ Visuals & Customization

The UI adapts to you, offering unparalleled aesthetic flexibility and design elements inspired by the best media apps on the market.



* **Dual Aesthetic Modes:** Choose between two sleek, high-end themes: an **Apple Music** style or a sharp **Spotify** look.
* **Dynamic Color Sync:** The player seamlessly syncs its color scheme with the **currently playing song's cover art**. If no cover is present, it intelligently uses your operating system's **native color scheme**.
* **Ultimate UI Personalization:** Fully customize the player's interface, including support for custom fonts like **San Francisco Pro** and **Poppins**.
* **Mini-Player Widget:** Instantly transform the main application into a small, floating **mini-player** inspired by the Spotify Android widget, perfect for multitasking.
* **Redesigned Full-Screen Experience:** Enjoy a full-screen mode reminiscent of the classic Windows Media Center (WMC), but completely **modernized with Spotify and Apple Music design language**.
* **Apple Music-Style Full-Screen Lyrics:** Dive into your music with a beautiful, distraction-free **full-screen lyrics display**.


### ðŸ’¿ Compatibility & Performance

Forget format restrictions. ProPlayer DELUXE handles virtually anything you throw at it while prioritizing original quality.



* **Unrivaled Media Support:** Supports **all** major video and image formats, including niche files like **BMP** and **HEIC** images.
* **True Quality Playback:** The player bypasses standard FFmpeg encoding limits, ensuring video playback utilizes the **original quality and speed** without unnecessary recompression.


### ðŸŽ§ Advanced Playback Tools

Enhance your listening and viewing experience with smart, powerful integrated tools.



* **Automatic Lyrics Finder:** For audio files, the app automatically attempts to find and display synchronized lyrics from platforms like Genius and Musixmatch.
* **Smart Subtitle Generation:** Use the automatic subtitle generator for video files, or load your own custom subtitle files in formats like **SRT** and others.
* **Sound Control:** Fine-tune your audio output with a built-in **Equalizer** and enjoy dynamic visual feedback with the **Visualizer**.
* **Library Management:** Easily create and manage custom **Playlists** and control your listening flow with comprehensive **Queues**.
